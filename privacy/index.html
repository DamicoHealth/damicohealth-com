1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Damico Health Inc. ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information when you use the DH Field EMR mobile and desktop applications (the "App").

DH Field EMR is designed exclusively for international medical outreach and global health programs. It is NOT intended for use within the United States healthcare system and is NOT a certified Electronic Health Record (EHR) system under any regulatory framework.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Damico Health Inc. does not access, view, or store the patient data you enter into the App. We do not operate any servers that hold patient medical records.

Specifically, we do NOT collect:

  • Patient names, dates of birth, or demographic information
  • Medical histories, diagnoses, or treatment information
  • Laboratory results or clinical data
  • Photos, documents, or other patient-related files
  • Location data beyond what Apple provides for crash reporting

3. How Data is Stored

Local Storage: All patient data entered into the App is stored locally on your device using Apple's Core Data framework.

Cloud Sync (Optional): If you enable cloud sync by creating or joining an Organization, your data is stored in Apple's CloudKit Public Database service. This data is partitioned by your Organization Code and is accessible only to devices that have been registered with that Organization Code.

Apple's Role: When cloud sync is enabled, data is transmitted to and stored by Apple's CloudKit service. Apple's privacy policy governs that storage. See apple.com/legal/privacy.

4. Information We Do Collect

We may collect limited information through standard Apple services:

  • Subscription Status: If you subscribe to a paid tier, Apple provides us with anonymous subscription receipt information to verify your entitlement.
  • Crash Reports: If the App crashes, Apple may send anonymous crash diagnostics to us through Apple's standard developer services.
  • App Store Analytics: Apple provides aggregate, anonymous App Store analytics (downloads, impressions) through App Store Connect.

5. Your Responsibilities

When using DH Field EMR, YOU and YOUR ORGANIZATION are responsible for:

  • Obtaining appropriate patient consent before collecting medical data
  • Complying with data protection laws in your country of operation
  • Securing devices that contain patient information
  • Maintaining adequate backups of your data
  • Controlling which devices are registered to your Organization
  • Appropriately handling, storing, and eventually deleting patient records

6. Subscriptions and Payments

Subscriptions are managed exclusively through Apple's App Store. We do not process payments directly. You can manage, pause, or cancel your subscription at any time through your Apple ID settings.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children. It is a professional tool used by medical personnel for the documentation of patient encounters, which may include patients of any age, but the App is operated by trained adults only.

8. International Data Transfers

If you use cloud sync, your data is stored on Apple's servers which may be located in various countries. You are responsible for ensuring compliance with cross-border data transfer laws applicable to your organization.

9. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Data stored locally on your device persists until you delete it from the device or uninstall the App.
  • Data stored in CloudKit persists until you delete it through the App or delete your Organization.
  • You may request that we help you delete your Organization and all associated data by emailing support@damicohealth.org.

10. Security

We use Apple's standard security practices for iOS, iPadOS, and macOS apps, including:

  • App Sandbox protection
  • HTTPS encryption for all network communications
  • CloudKit's built-in encryption for cloud-stored data
  • Hardware-backed encryption on supported devices

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted to this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Disclaimers

DH Field EMR IS NOT A MEDICAL DEVICE. It is a data entry and record-keeping tool only. All clinical decisions must be made by qualified healthcare professionals. Damico Health Inc. assumes no liability for clinical decisions made using data from this application.

THIS SOFTWARE IS NOT HIPAA COMPLIANT. It is not intended for use within the United States healthcare system or any other jurisdiction requiring certified electronic health record systems.
