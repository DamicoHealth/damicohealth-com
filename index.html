The problem

Paper Forms Don't Scale

Medical outreach teams see hundreds of patients per clinic day. By week's end, a stack of paper forms sits waiting to be entered, referrals need tracking, and donors need reports. Commercial EMRs cost thousands per year, assume constant internet, and are built for hospital workflows — not field clinics.

📝

Paper forms get lost

Lost, damaged, or take weeks to enter after returning from the field.

🌍

Remote locations

Unreliable internet makes cloud-only EMRs impossible to use on-site.

💰

Commercial EMRs

Cost $5,000 to $20,000 per year — out of reach for most outreach teams.

⚕️

Generic systems

Don't match global health workflows, triage patterns, or donor reporting.

The approach

Built for the Field

DH Field EMR is designed for how medical outreach actually works. Enter a patient in seconds, keep working with zero internet, and sync everyone's records to your own cloud at the end of the day.

⚡

Fast Entry

Single-page encounter form with one-tap buttons for complaints, diagnoses, and medications. A full record in well under a minute.

📡

Offline-First

Runs entirely on the device — no signal required. When you reconnect, records sync to your cloud automatically.

🎯

Make It Yours

Build your own form sections and fields, set up multiple encounter types, and edit every preset to match your clinic. It's open source — change anything you like.

Capabilities

Everything You Need to Run a Field Clinic

From patient registration through donor reporting — without the billing modules and hospital overhead you don't.

Patient Management

  • Auto-generated MRN from name and date of birth
  • Automatic return-patient detection & carry-forward
  • Multi-visit history per patient
  • Searchable records with advanced filters

Clinical Documentation

  • Vitals, history, and chief complaint
  • Configurable lab tests (positive/negative or numeric)
  • Urinalysis dipstick support
  • Diagnosis presets with free-text override
  • Medication builder with dosing math
  • Procedures, imaging, and surgical encounters

Customize the Form

  • Add your own sections and questions
  • Field types: text, choices, numbers, ranges, yes/no, dates
  • Multiple encounter forms (e.g. general, surgical, dental)
  • Editable presets: meds, diagnoses, sites, providers

Reporting

  • Live analytics dashboard
  • Disease-burden charts & lab positivity rates
  • Provider productivity
  • One-tap donor report
  • CSV export for any spreadsheet or stats tool

Your Data, Your Cloud

  • Syncs to a free Supabase project that you own
  • Multiple devices share one organization's records
  • Works fully standalone (offline-only) too
  • Download a complete backup file anytime

Open & Free

  • Free forever — no subscription, no per-seat fees
  • Full source code on GitHub
  • Self-host and customize for your team
  • No vendor lock-in: it's your data and your code
Runs anywhere

Works on the Devices You Already Have

It's a web app — open it in a browser, then "Add to Home Screen" to install it like a normal app. No app store, no special hardware.

📱

iPad & iPhone

Open in Safari, then Share → Add to Home Screen. Touch-optimized for triage and provider stations.

🤖

Android

Open in Chrome and install when prompted. Great on low-cost field tablets.

💻

Laptop & Desktop

Chrome or Edge on Windows, Mac, or ChromeOS — ideal for an end-of-day data and reporting station.

Same app, same data — every device syncs through your own cloud.

Who it's for

Designed for Global Health Work

DH Field EMR is built for medical outreach programs operating in resource-limited international settings. Typical deployments include:

  • Short-term medical mission trips
  • Mobile clinic programs
  • NGO outreach campaigns
  • Disaster response medical teams
  • Rural health outreach
  • Medical school international electives

⚠️ Please Read: Intended Use & Disclaimers

DH Field EMR is a documentation and record-keeping tool only. It is not a medical device, it does not provide medical advice, and it must never replace the judgment of a qualified healthcare professional. Dosing helpers and presets are conveniences that you are responsible for verifying.

Designed for international medical outreach — not the US healthcare system. It is not a certified Electronic Health Record and is not HIPAA-compliant. Organizations are responsible for complying with the data-protection laws of their country of operation and for obtaining appropriate patient consent.

You are responsible for your own data. When you connect your own cloud (Supabase), your patient data lives in your account under your control — keep backups and secure your credentials. The software is provided free and "as is," without warranty of any kind. See the Terms and Privacy Policy for full details.
