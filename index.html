The problem

Paper Forms Don't Scale

Medical outreach teams see hundreds of patients per clinic day. By week's end, a stack of paper forms sits waiting to be entered, referrals need tracking, and donors need reports. Traditional EMR systems cost thousands per year, require constant internet, and are designed for hospital workflows — not field clinics.

📝

Paper forms get lost

Lost, damaged, or take weeks to enter after returning from the field.

🌍

Remote locations

Unreliable internet makes cloud-only EMRs impossible to use on-site.

💰

Commercial EMRs

Cost $5,000 to $20,000 per year — out of reach for most outreach teams.

⚕️

Generic systems

Don't match global health workflows, triage patterns, or donor reporting.

The solution

Built for the Field

DH Field EMR is designed for how medical outreach actually works. Enter paper forms in seconds, not minutes. Sync across every device at the end of the day. Generate donor reports with one tap.

⚡

Fast Entry

Single-page encounter form. One-click buttons for everything. Enter a complete patient record in 30 seconds.

📡

Offline-First

Works completely offline. Devices sync automatically when internet is available. Perfect for remote clinics.

🎯

Customizable

Edit every preset — medications, diagnoses, procedures, sites, providers. Make it fit your organization.

Who it's for

Designed for Global Health Work

DH Field EMR is built for medical outreach programs operating in resource-limited international settings. Typical deployments include:

"We entered 300 patients from a 4-day clinic in under 2 hours. The old system would have taken us all week."

  • Short-term medical mission trips
  • Mobile clinic programs
  • NGO outreach campaigns
  • Disaster response medical teams
  • Rural health outreach
  • Medical school international electives

⚠️ Global Health Use Only

This software is designed exclusively for international medical outreach and is NOT intended for use within the United States healthcare system.

DH Field EMR is not a certified Electronic Health Record system and is not compliant with HIPAA or any US healthcare regulations. Organizations using this software are responsible for complying with data protection laws in their country of operation and obtaining appropriate patient consent.

This software is a data entry and record-keeping tool only. All clinical decisions must be made by qualified healthcare professionals.
